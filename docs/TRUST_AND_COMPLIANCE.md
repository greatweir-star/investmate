# TRUST AND COMPLIANCE

## Positioning Boundary

InvestMate is an AI decision support tool, not an investment advisor, broker, asset manager, or trading system.

## Required Language Rules

Avoid:

- guaranteed return;
- “must buy”;
- “will rise”;
- “risk-free”;
- precise profit promises.

Prefer:

- “evidence suggests”;
- “risk is elevated”;
- “consider waiting”;
- “suitable only if…”;
- “not enough evidence”.

## Trust Mechanisms

1. Evidence transparency
2. Historical decision record
3. Confidence score
4. Invalidation conditions
5. User feedback loop

This output is generated for educational and decision-support purposes only. It is not financial advice, brokerage advice, or a guarantee of future performance. You remain responsible for all investment decisions.
